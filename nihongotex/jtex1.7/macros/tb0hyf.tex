\title Hyphenation Exception Log
\\ \endx

\pagexref{hyphenlog}

Below is a list of words that \TeX\ fails to hyphenate properly.
It last appeared in Volume~5, No.~1, on page~15.
Everything listed there is repeated here.

As expected, there are more instances of missed than of incorrect
hyphens.  The ``\TeX'' column gives results from |\showhyphens{...}|;
hyphens.  |\showhyphens{...}| produced the results in the ``\TeX'' column.
entries in the second column are suitable for inclusion in a
|\hyphenation{...}| list.

In most instances, inflected forms are not shown for nouns and verbs;
however, all forms should be specified in a |\hyphenation{...}| list
if they might occur in your document.
